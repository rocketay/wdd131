Site Plan

Site Name

Speaky is a website for storytelling and public speaking courses.

Logo:

I chose this name because it is short, memorable, and directly relates to speaking and storytelling skills.

Optional domain: speaky.com or speakycourses.com

Site Purpose

Speaky provides information about storytelling courses, including course descriptions, schedules, pricing, instructor details, and student testimonials. Visitors can also contact the team and submit an interest/signup form.

Scenarios

  • What storytelling course is best for beginners?
  • How can I improve my public speaking confidence?
  • Are the courses available online or in person?
  • How much do the courses cost?
